Ittehad RCC Pipe Factory is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality Reinforced Cement Concrete (RCC) pipes in Islamabad, Pakistan. With over a decade of experience, we have been a trusted partner for both government and private sector projects, delivering durable, reliable, and precision-engineered products.

We are committed to quality, safety, and timely delivery, ensuring every project is completed with excellence. Our pipes are designed to meet ASTM C-76 and BS 5911 standards, making us the preferred choice for critical infrastructure projects.
