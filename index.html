About Us

Dedicated to building strong foundations for the future

Our Commitment to Excellence

Established over 15 years ago, Ittehad RCC Pipe Factory has grown to become one of the most trusted manufacturers and suppliers of Reinforced Cement Concrete (RCC) Pipes in the Islamabad-Rawalpindi region. Our factory, strategically located in Tarnol, serves as a hub for supplying high-quality construction materials to major projects across the twin cities and beyond.

We take pride in our extensive experience supplying to prestigious projects including DHA, Bahria Town, Capital Smart City, Blue World City, and government departments such as WASA and Public Health. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for contractors, developers, and government agencies alike.
