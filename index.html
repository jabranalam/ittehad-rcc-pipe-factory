About Us

Ittehad RCC Pipe Factory has been a trusted manufacturer of Reinforced Concrete (RCC) pipes since 2008. We specialize in producing pipes for drainage, sewage, irrigation, and construction projects, ensuring durability and compliance with industry standards.

Our factory is located at Tarnol Pattak, F-15, Jammu Kashmir Housing Scheme, Islamabad, Pakistan. With years of experience, we pride ourselves on quality craftsmanship and timely delivery.
